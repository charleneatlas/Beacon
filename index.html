How To Play

  • MAKE SURE YOUR SOUND IS ON
  • Click to start experience.
  • Spacebar to play/pause playback.
  • Left/Right Arrows to browse planetary system patterns.
  • Up/Down Arrows to browse star systems.

Planet Classification Scheme

  • sub-Neptune: Any planet with R⊕ between 1.9 and 2.9. Otherwise...
  • Terrestrial: M⊕ < 2 and R⊕ <= 1.6
  • Low-mass sub-Neptune: M⊕ < 2 and R⊕ > 1.6
  • Super-Earth: M⊕ < 10
  • Neptune-like: M⊕ < 60
  • Gas Giant: M⊕ < 300
  • Brown Dwarf: M⊕ > 300

Unit Glossary

  • R⊕: A unit for counting in Earth radii. Earth's radius is 6371 KM.
  • M⊕: A unit for counting in Earth masses. Earth's mass is 5.9722 x 1024 kg.
