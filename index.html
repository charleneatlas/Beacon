Beacon

Experience the diversity of exoplanet systems through rhythmic patterns.

Created by Charlene Atlas.

App GitHub Data Notebook Process More from Charlene Atlas

This app is focused on the ~100 multi-planet star systems with confirmed exoplanets within the TESS dataset.

Uses TESS data publicly available from the Mikulski Archive for Space Telescopes (MAST).

How To Play

  • MAKE SURE YOUR SOUND IS ON
  • Click to start/stop the experience.
  • Spacebar to change planetary system.
