Beacon

Experience the diversity of exoplanet systems through rhythmic patterns.

Created by Charlene Atlas.

App GitHub Data Notebook Process More from Charlene Atlas

Uses data publicly available from the NASA Exoplanet Science Institute.

How To Play

  • MAKE SURE YOUR SOUND IS ON
  • Click to start experience.
  • Spacebar to play/pause playback.
  • Left/Right Arrows to browse planetary system patterns.
  • Up/Down Arrows to browse star systems within the active pattern.
  • TAB to toggle on/off our own solar system.
